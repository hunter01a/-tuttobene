Our history

Forty years side by side

1982

Once upon a time there was Bar Tucano

Marco Pasquini and Claudio Pecchioli begin their adventure at Calenzano, as coffee shop pioneers in the suburbs of Firenze. In years when the bar was just a meeting point, they want to bring beauty, taste and well-being — quick lunches for workers, attention to aesthetics, uncompromising quality.

~2000

Tuttobene is born

The turning point: the first TuttoBene restaurant opens in the Campi Bisenzio industrial area. A large bar-pastry shop-bistro with its own production - from breakfast pastries to leavened products, from the delicatessen to the fresh pasta laboratory - in an industrial style but tasteful environment.

2002

The Gambero Rosso

Tuttobene enters the Bar d'Italia guide of Gambero Rosso among the best bars in the country. Ten years later the coveted star also arrives, and over the years the three cups and three beans: the highest recognition. «Objective achieved thanks also to our collaborators», says Marco.

Today

A local family

The group has six venues in the Florentine hinterland, each self-managed but linked by the TB brand and the same philosophy. The last chapter: helping the historic collaborators to open a place of their own, on Bar Quotidiano, towards Prato.

TB is a way of being that transforms into thinking, creating and cooking in the best possible way what we care about most: goodness. Our ingredients? Mind, heart and good taste.

— Philosophy Tuttobene

Italian art, every day

The Italian cappuccino

Selected blend, milk whipped into a fine, shiny cream, served at the right temperature. The ritual of the Italian breakfast, performed with the care that Corriere has defined as among the best ever.

Pastries & leavened products

About twenty leavened products every morning: brioches, focaccias, cremini, bedside rugs, cream puff pastries. All of our own production, from the laboratory directly to the showcase.

Tuscan gastronomy & cuisine

Tuscan dishes cooked on site, a very popular lunch spot, a wine shop for true connoisseurs and a shop selling refined products to bring the Tuttobene home.

Parties, aperitifs & catering

From the bee to the buffet, from catering to delivery: the large spaces with an industrial-chic design lend themselves to birthdays, business meetings and anniversaries. On the terrace or in the living room.
